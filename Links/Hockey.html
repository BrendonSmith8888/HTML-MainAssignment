Hockey

Field hockey, also called hockey, outdoor game played by two opposing teams of 11 players each who use sticks curved at the striking end to hit a small, hard ball into their opponent's goal.

Why I love playing hockey is because the amount of training and range of skillsets you need to learn to become a top hockey player. Again it's also a team sport, but for individual reasons it keeps me fit and I've enjoyed how the much the game has evovled over the years in becoming one of the most exciting sports to watch.
